​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We get info in a few ways:

a. **Stuff You Tell Us**

Like your name, email, number, where to send stuff.

Also, if you make an account, we keep your login info safe.

Anything else you tell us - feedback, surveys, etc.

b. **Automatic Info**

Your IP address, what kind of browser/device you're using

What pages you look at, how long you stay, what you click

Where you came from (another website, search engine).

c. **Cookies &amp; Tracking**

We use cookies (small files) to:

Make the site work better for you

Remember your settings

See what's popular on the site

Show you ads that might interest you (if that's part of the deal)

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but some stuff might not work right.

d. **Other Companies**

Other companies help us with stuff like understanding how people use the site (Google Analytics, for example), showing ads, and letting you share to social media. They might grab some info too.

**2. How We Use Your Info**

We use your info to:

Keep the website running and make it better

Answer your questions and help you out

Send emails with news or deals (if you sign up)

See what people like, what's not working, and fix it

Stop bad guys from doing bad things

Obey the law

**3. Who We Share With**

We don't sell your info. But we might share it with:

*   **Service people:** Companies that help us run the site, process payments, or understand how people use the site.
*   **Law people:** If a judge tells us we have to, or if it's needed to protect ourselves or others.
*   **New owners:** If we get bought out or sell the company.
*   **If you say it's okay:** If you give us the go-ahead.

4. **Links to Other Sites**

We might link to other sites, but we're not in charge of their privacy stuff. Read their policies before giving them any info.

**5. How Long We Keep Your Stuff**

We only keep your info as long as we need it for the reasons above, or if the law says we have to. After that, we delete it or scramble it so no one knows it's you.

**6. Your Rights**

Depending on where you live, you can:

*   See what info we have about you and update it
*   Ask us to delete your info
*   Tell us to stop using your info in some ways
*   Unsubscribe from marketing emails

To do any of that, email us at [contact email].

**7. How We Keep Your Info Safe**

We do our best to protect your info from hackers, accidents, etc. But the internet isn't 100% safe.

**8. International Peeps**

If you're visiting from another country, your info might be sent to [your country], where the privacy laws might be different. By using the site, you're okay with that.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

This site isn't for kids under [13/16]. We don't try to get info from kids. If we find out we have a kid's info, we'll delete it ASAP.

**10. Cookies &amp; Analytics 

We use tools to see how people use the site:

*   What pages they go to
*   How long they stay
*   What links they click

You can usually turn off these tools in your browser settings.

**11. Email Stuff**

If you sign up for emails, we might send you deals or news. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in the email or contacting us.

**12. Policy Changes**

We might change this policy sometimes. We'll post the new version on this page with a new Effective Date. Check back now and then.
